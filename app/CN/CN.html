Traditional / folk music of Canada

Each country may has different types of folks musics. From town to town, village by village this can be changed. Different music instrument or different style of folk music maybe used. Please help us to improve the FolkCloud and send us some folk songs from your country. You can upload folk songs by clicking on 'Post a Song' on the top. Also if you can write an article about folk music of Canada, we can put your article on folkcloud with your name.

On the list below you can find some folk songs or traditional songs from Canada. Each song has some tags which specify its genres or musical instrument which is used in that song. Inside each page you can find more information about the artist or music genre. You can listen to all songs as playlist too or you can add each song to your personal playlist.
