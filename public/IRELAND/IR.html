Traditional / folk music of Ireland

Each country may has different types of folks musics. From town to town, village by village this can be changed. Different music instrument or different style of folk music maybe used. Please help us to improve the SingCloud and send us some folk songs from your country. You can upload folk songs by clicking on 'Upload a song' on the top. Also if you can write an article about folk music of Ireland, we can put your article on folkcloud with your name.
