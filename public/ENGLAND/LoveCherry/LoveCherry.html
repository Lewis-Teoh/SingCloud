I Gave My A Love Cherry - Carly Simon

This old English lullaby, also known as "The Riddle Song," is from the album "JOAN BAEZ in San Francisco" which was recorded when she was 17years old.

It's a clumsy recording with uneven volume, yet her rendition of the song is the most beautiful and haunting version you'll ever hear.

Anyone who wants to know more about Joan Baez should read her candid 1987 autobiography titled, "And A Voice To Sing With."
